Registration Details


ICAMMAE-2025 is sponsored by All India Council for Technical Education, New Delhi under AICTE VAANI (Vibrant Advocacy for Advancement and Nurturing of Indian Languages) Scheme. The registration fee for attending the conference is Free therefore not applicable to the participants. However, the travel expenses and expenses for stay at REVA University are to be borne by the participants.

The Participants of the conference will be provided working meals, copy of abstracts of conference proceeding and a conference dinner.
