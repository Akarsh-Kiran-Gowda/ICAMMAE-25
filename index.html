9th International Conference on Advances in Mechanical, Mechatronics, and Aerospace Engineering

13th & 14th August 2025 | REVA University, Bangalore


ICAMMAE 2025

ICAMMAE 2025 is a prestigious platform designed to bring together a diverse group of academicians, researchers, industry professionals, and students to discuss and explore recent interdisciplinary trends in Mechanical, Mechatronics and Aerospace Engineering. This international conference aims to foster collaboration and innovation across different disciplines, contributing to the advancement of knowledge and technology in this rapidly evolving industrial landscape. All the selected papers will be published in Scopus indexed journals.

Call for Papers

Topics of Interest Include but are not Limited to:

  • Materials: Composites, Polymers, Ceramics, Superalloys, Functionally Graded Materials, Sustainable Materials, Smart Materials etc.,
  • Mechanical Engineering: Additive Manufacturing (3D Printing), Robotics and Automation, Thermal and Fluid Sciences,Finite Element Analysis (FEA), Smart Materials and Structures, Sustainable Engineering, Vibration and Acoustics, Manufacturing Technologies, Biomechanics and Bioengineering.
  • Mechatronics Engineering: Mechatronic System Integration, Industrial Automation and Control, Embedded Systems and IoT, Autonomous and Intelligent Vehicles, Sensors/Transducrs and Actuators, Machine Vision System, Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) in Mechatronics, Human-Robot Interaction (HRI), Energy Harvesting and Power Management, MEMS, Nano Sensors and Actuators.
  • Aerospace Engineering: Aerodynamics and Fluid Mechanics, Propulsion Systems, Space Systems Engineering, Materials for Aerospace Applications, Aircraft Design and Systems, Flight Control and Navigation Systems, Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV’s) and Drones, Space Robotics and Automation, Aerospace Structural Design, Advanced Avionics and Communication Systems, Artificial Intelligence in Aviation, Sustainable Aviation and Aerospace.
